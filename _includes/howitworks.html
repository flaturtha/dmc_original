How it works:

Every month, we'll send you a carefully selected, reviewed and approved mystery novel for your reading enjoyment. Just download your prefered format to you favorite e-reader device or app, and relax with a good book.

On the 1st of each month, you'll receive an email with the subject line $1 Mystery Club [MONTH] Selection, containing a brief description and cover image of that month's selection.

If you want to read it, click the Read button in the email and we'll process your monthly $1 charge, then redirect you to the download page. There, select your preferred format and download. Instructions for loading on various devices will be provided.

Not interested in this month's selection? Just click Pass and your credit card will NOT be charged (but fair warning … if you decide later that you do want to read this novel, you'll have to buy it at full price, up to $5.99.

Either way, your satisfaction is guaranteed. If you don't enjoy the month's selection, we'll refund your $1 and send you a different novel.
